Game Design

Overview

My name is Freya and I am currently a 2nd year student at Wyke Sixth Form College. I have a passion for Games Design and I have designed this portfolio to show my interest in the subject. My portfolio has been split into four sections, each with the intent of showing a different aspect of game design.

The first section is titled 'Cliffhanger' and covers a large project which aims to show how I can take a concept and develop it into a prototype game. The second section, titled 'Crown of Shadows' aims to show level and environment design as well as some narrative design. The third section is titled 'Notes' and contains a collection of game ideas and concepts, showing how I conceptualise a game. The final section is titled 'Games' and covers general problem solving and programming concepts.



About Me

I've grown up loving video games, starting with playing Crash Bandicoot 3 on our PS2 with my dad. Throughout my childhood, my hobby has grown into a passion. Some of my favourite games include The Last of Us, Baldur's Gate 3 and Death Stranding, I love narrative and level desgin which these games expertly display. Having played many games in the past, I have started to understand and appreciate the behind-the-scenes work that goes into making a game. Throughout my time at college, I have developed skills and projects which show my understanding of Games Design, all of which have been used to create this portfolio.
