This page contains pages from my notebook which I write ideas for games in. This simply shows some of the ideas I have had for games I would like to create in the future. The goal of this is to try and develop a basic game idea into a more fleshed out concept. The slideshow of images contains:

  1. A concept for a platformer inspired by 'The Forgotten Realms' from Dungeons and Dragons. This was the initial concept for my Computer Science NEA.
  2. Developing the concept further by planning themes of levels and features to include.
  3. A concept for a road trip game inspired by Road 96, brainstorming ideas for story concepts then expanding on the chosen idea. Planning simple mechanics for a story-oriented concept.
  4. Conceptualising a game based off an idea for a game mechanic. Developing environment and story concepts using the idea of a camera that allows the player to view the same area in the past.
  5. A concept for a city-builder strategy game inspired by games such as Frostpunk. Planning potential features and obstacles for the player and how the environment and gameplay may change over time.
